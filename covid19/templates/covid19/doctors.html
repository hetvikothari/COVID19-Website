What doctors say..

If you have mild symptoms, you may recover at home. You'll likely be asked to isolate yourself as much as possible from family and pets while you're sick, wear a mask when you're around people and pets, and use a separate bedroom and bathroom. It is recommended that you stay in home isolation for a period of time except to get medical care. Follow guidelines from your doctor and local health department about when you can end home isolation. If you're very ill, you may need to be treated in the hospital.
